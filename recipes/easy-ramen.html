Ingredients

  • cooked chicken thighs, i used frozen korean fried chicken
  • egg noodles
  • eggs
  • spring onions
  • dumplings (because i like it)
  • chicken stock
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • soy sauce
  • chilli oil
  • msg
  • sesame oil
  • black pepper
  • frozen dumplings

    • Instructions

    1. Chop garlic, ginger, and spring onions.
    2. In a pan, add sesame oil. Fry garlic, ginger, and white part of spring onions until aromatic.
    3. Add chicken stock, soy sauce, chilli oil, black pepper. Taste and add more if necessary. Boil for 10-15 mins.
    4. Cook egg noodles, chicken thighs, and frozen dumplings according to package instructions. Make soft-boiled eggs by boiling for 6 minutes.
    5. Now, finally time to assemble. Take chicken stock in a bowl, add your noodles. Top with sliced chicken, dumplings, jammy eggs. Garnish with spring onions and chilli oil as preferred.
